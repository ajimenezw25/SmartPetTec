{# ── SECTION 3: How it works ── #}

ℹ️ How it works

Refill cycle
When the water level drops below the low threshold, the servo opens for 2 seconds then closes automatically. Each complete open→close cycle counts as one refill.
Counter alerts
At 1 refill remaining: warning notification. At 0 refills: critical alert and the valve is locked until you press Reset above.
Sensor config sync
After saving, go to the device Command Panel and click Sync Config to push the distance calibration to the ESP32 over MQTT.
Manual refill
Use Refill Now in the Command Panel to trigger an immediate refill cycle (also decrements the counter).
