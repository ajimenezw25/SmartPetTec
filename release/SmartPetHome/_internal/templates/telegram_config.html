📱 Telegram Configuration

What are Telegram alerts?

SmartPetTec can send you instant notifications via Telegram when your devices detect problems — like low food, critical food levels, or device errors. You only need to link your Telegram account once.

How to set up

  1. Open the SmartPetTec bot on Telegram.
  2. Press Start or send /start.
  3. The bot will reply with your Telegram Chat ID.
  4. Copy that number.
  5. Paste it into the form on the right.
  6. Click Save.
✈️ Open @smartpettec_alerts_bot
