🍽️ Diet Mode & Thresholds

Complete Bowl

Fills the bowl to maximum capacity at each scheduled time. Best for pets that self-regulate. The feeder tops up whatever the weight sensor shows is missing.

Redistribute Daily Diet

Splits a total daily ration across all enabled schedules. If the pet skips a meal (leftover detected), the next portion is reduced proportionally. Best for portion-controlled diets.
